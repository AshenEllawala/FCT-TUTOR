Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a tutor for my course?

Go to "Find Tutors" page, browse available subjects, and click on your course to see qualified tutors with ratings and availability.

How do I register for tutoring sessions?

Click Register, select "I'm a Student", fill in your details, then browse tutors and book sessions directly from their profiles.

Can I reschedule or cancel a session?

Yes! Go to "My Sessions", find your upcoming session, and click "Reschedule" to select a new time or contact support for cancellations.

What subjects does FCT Tutor cover?

Mathematics for Technology, Physics for Technology, Structured Programming, Computer Networks, and Statics for Technology.

How do I become a tutor on the platform?

Click Register, select "I'm a Tutor", complete the application with your qualifications and expertise. We'll verify and approve your profile.

Where do tutoring sessions take place?

Sessions are held on campus in designated study rooms, libraries, or labs. Location details are provided when you book a session.
